About Me

Hello! I am Fanxin He, a creative designer passionate about packaging, branding, typography, illustration, and web design.

I’m a designer who enjoys creating clear and visually appealing work. I’m interested in branding, typography, layout design, and digital illustration. I especially like traditional-style design and typographic posters. I enjoy mixing classic design elements with modern ideas, using typography, color, and composition to create feeling and meaning. My style is mostly clean and minimal, but I also like using bold colors to add contrast and energy. In my work, I try to combine tradition with new ideas while showing my own personality and creativity. Also, I enjoy traveling and taking photos and these interests often inspire new ideas in my design work
